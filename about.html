About  SAT/ACT Sense


SAT/ACT Sense was established in 2008 by Steven Rossner, a former high school English teacher, who had also worked for a number of private test prep companies. After 15 years of tutoring students for the SAT and ACT, Steve felt it was time to launch his own test prep company.

Steve’s goal is to distinguish SAT/ACT Sense from the cookie cutter programs that many high schools and test prep companies offer. Instead, we use a more customized approach based on three criteria: the student’s personality, knowledge base, and level of commitment. In order to effectively match each student with the right tutor, Steve personally meets with each student and his/her family before the start of tutoring.
